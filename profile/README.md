HeartLink is a group-centric SNS platform that breaks down intergenerational communication barriers through a voice interface. Its real-time group feed feature converts AI voice recognition into text and generates images based on that text, allowing users to easily share their daily lives through simple voice inputs. This is particularly beneficial for elderly users who are less familiar with digital devices, enabling them to communicate naturally. Accessible through LG StandbyME, HeartLink combines cutting-edge AI technology with the warmth of family and friendship connections.
